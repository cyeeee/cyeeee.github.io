About

This is Chenyi, an engineer, developer, and designer.

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Integrated Design and Media from NYU, and have a background in computer engineering.

I like the combination of design and technology and am interested in creative and novel applications of technology. I am always curious about the relationship and interaction between humans and technology. I enjoy applying my knowledge and logical thinking to solve complex problems to fulfill needs while learning new things along the way.

I am also very collaborative and comfortable working with people with different mindsets to gain various insights. I enjoy the collision of ideas and the process of solving problems and needs by communicating with people.

Thank you for visiting my space, get in touch if interested!
